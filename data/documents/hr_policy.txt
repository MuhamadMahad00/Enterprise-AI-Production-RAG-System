TEYZIX CORE HR POLICY

All employees are expected to maintain professional conduct during working hours.

Office timings are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

Employees are entitled to 20 annual paid leaves per calendar year.

Remote work is allowed for approved employees based on project requirements.

Emergency leave requests must be reported to HR before the start of the workday.

Employees must maintain confidentiality regarding company data and client information.

Use of unauthorized software or external storage devices is prohibited on company systems.

Performance evaluations are conducted quarterly by team managers.

Employees must comply with cybersecurity and password management policies.

Two-factor authentication is mandatory for all internal platforms.

Harassment, discrimination, or workplace misconduct will result in disciplinary action.

All employees are required to attend mandatory compliance and security training sessions.

Company laptops must not be shared with unauthorized individuals.

Employees are encouraged to report suspicious emails or phishing attempts immediately.

Internal communication should be conducted using approved company tools only.

Violation of company policies may lead to suspension or termination based on severity.
