TEYZIX CORE EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING GUIDE

All new employees must complete onboarding within their first week.

Employees receive official company credentials from the IT department.

Access to internal platforms is granted after identity verification.

New hires are required to configure multi-factor authentication during account setup.

Employees must review HR policies and cybersecurity guidelines before system access.

Development team members are assigned GitHub repositories and project environments.

All employees must install approved security and productivity software.

Initial training includes project workflow, communication standards, and deployment procedures.

Daily stand-up meetings are conducted every morning at 10:00 AM.

Employees should submit weekly progress updates to their team lead.

The engineering team follows Agile-based sprint workflows for project management.

Code reviews are mandatory before merging changes into the production branch.

Internal documentation is maintained inside the company knowledge portal.

Employees are encouraged to use reusable and modular code practices.

Support requests must be submitted through the internal ticketing system.

New employees receive technical mentors during the probation period.
